Luxury Travel PR & Marketing in Asia-Pacific

Leading travel PR agency in Sydney with deep regional expertise across APAC

Asia-Pacific is one of the most dynamic regions in global travel, home to some of the world’s fastest-growing luxury markets. From Australia and New Zealand to Singapore, Japan and the South Pacific, international brands need Australian travel PR agencies who can navigate cultural nuances, connect with influential media, and build resonance with discerning travellers.

Based in Sydney, our Asia-Pacific team combines international perspective with local knowledge. We specialise in luxury travel PR alongside integrated trade marketing and digital campaigns, helping hotels, airlines, cruise lines and destinations cut through in a highly competitive space.

What we do for luxury travel brands in Asia-Pacific

Every brand we represent across APAC has different ambitions, whether it’s building recognition in Australia, driving bookings from India, or engaging trade partners across the region.

By uniting PR, trade and digital under one roof, we deliver strategies that not only build awareness but also drive measurable luxury travel PR results across Asia-Pacific.

  • Creative campaigns that generate powerful media coverage, from destination storytelling to hotel and airline launches.

  • Tailored programmes that connect brands with agents, tour operators and partners across APAC.

  • Social media, influencer partnerships and digital campaigns designed to expand reach and engagement.
