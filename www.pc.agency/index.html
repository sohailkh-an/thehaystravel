The Agency

The Hays Travel Agency is one of the world’s most sought-after luxury travel PR and trade marketing consultancies, working with the most exciting major players and emerging travel brands around the globe. Our teams based in UK and Europe, Asia Pacific and the Middle East, and The Americas deliver high-impact consumer, corporate, trade marketing and digital campaigns that build reputations, tap into new markets and drive sales. We are also experts at crisis communications campaigns to support our clients when things don’t go as planned.

We work with luxury hotels, airlines, tourism boards, tour operators and cruise lines to create bold and memorable campaigns which build greater brand value over the long-term.

Our expert team has substantial experience in-house and agency-side, helping brands to find their niche and bring their personality to life. We showcase client success to the outside world, putting you firmly on the radar of media, influencers or other stakeholders.
