Luxury Travel PR & Marketing in the US

Global consultancy, local connections - building brand stories from New York across North America

The US remains one of the most influential travel markets, setting trends and shaping demand worldwide. With our office in New York City, we support luxury hotels, airlines, cruise lines and destinations in building visibility and credibility across North America.

As a trusted travel PR agency in NYC, we blend creativity with strategy, ensuring every campaign is designed to capture the attention of top-tier US media, trade partners and travellers. Alongside PR, we provide integrated trade marketing and digital communications, making us a full-service partner for brands entering or expanding in the US market.

What we do for luxury travel brands in the US

From iconic city hotels and coastal resorts to destination-wide campaigns, our work in the US celebrates the country’s unrivalled diversity of experiences.

By combining PR, trade and digital, we craft campaigns that elevate brands, capture the imagination of high-value travellers, and ultimately, inspire bookings across the United States.

  • Media relations and storytelling that secure coverage in outlets such as Travel & Leisure, Bloomberg and Condè Nast Traveler.

  • Engaging US travel advisors, operators and distributors through targeted programmes that drive bookings.

  • Social media, influencer partnerships and digital campaigns designed to expand reach and engagement.
